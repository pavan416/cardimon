Products

CARDAMOM PODS

Cardamom pods are aromatic spice seeds used in cooking and beverages.

CARDAMOM SEEDS

Cardamom seeds are small, flavorful seeds found within the pods of the cardamom plant, commonly used in cooking and baking.

CARDAMOM HUSK

Cardamom husk is the outer, inedible layer of the cardamom pod that is typically removed before using the seeds or ground cardamom in cooking.

CARDAMOM OIL

Cardamom oil is an essential oil extracted from cardamom seeds and is used for flavoring, aromatherapy, and medicinal purposes.

Requirements

The Requisites We Hold

Large Cardamom Pods

These are the largest and most mature pods, typically measuring around 1 to 1.5 inches in length. They often have a strong flavor and are preferred for some culinary applications.

Medium Cardamom Pods

Medium-sized pods are usually about 0.75 to 1 inch in length. They are versatile and commonly used in cooking and baking.

Small Cardamom Pods

Small cardamom pods are typically around 0.5 to 0.75 inches long. They are favored for their delicate flavor and are used in various dishes and beverages.

Extra Small Cardamom Pods

These are the smallest cardamom pods, measuring less than 0.5 inches in length. They are often used in recipes where a subtle cardamom flavor is desired.

Things Have To Know

Is It Healthy ?

  • Digestive Aid: Cardamom has traditionally been used to promote digestion and alleviate digestive issues such as gas, bloating, and indigestion.
  • Antioxidant Properties: Cardamom is rich in antioxidants, which help protect the body against oxidative stress and damage caused by free radicals
  • Anti-inflammatory Effects: Some studies suggest that cardamom may have anti-inflammatory properties, which could potentially benefit conditions related to inflammation, such as arthritis.
  • Oral Health: Cardamom is known for its antimicrobial properties and has been used in traditional medicine for oral health, including freshening breath and reducing oral infections.
  • Heart Health: Some studies have indicated that cardamom may have positive effects on cholesterol levels, potentially reducing the risk of heart disease.
  • Blood Sugar Control: Preliminary research suggests that cardamom may help improve blood sugar control by increasing insulin sensitivity and reducing insulin resistance.
